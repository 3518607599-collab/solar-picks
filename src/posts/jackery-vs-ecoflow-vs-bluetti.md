Jackery, EcoFlow, and Bluetti dominate the portable solar generator market. But which brand deserves your money? We've tested products from all three extensively, and here's our honest comparison.

## Brand Overview

### Jackery — The Reliable Veteran
- **Founded:** 2012
- **Strength:** Reliability, brand trust, ecosystem
- **Weakness:** Premium pricing, slower innovation
- **Battery tech:** LiFePO4 (newer models), Li-ion (older)
- **Warranty:** 3-5 years (model dependent)

Jackery was the first company to make portable solar generators mainstream. Their products aren't always the most feature-packed, but they work exactly as promised, every time.

### EcoFlow — The Innovation Leader
- **Founded:** 2017
- **Strength:** Fastest charging, best app, cutting-edge features
- **Weakness:** Newer brand, less long-term track record
- **Battery tech:** LiFePO4 (all new models)
- **Warranty:** 5 years

EcoFlow consistently pushes the envelope with features like X-Stream fast charging (0-80% in under an hour), modular battery expansion, and the best mobile app in the industry.

### Bluetti — The Value Champion
- **Founded:** 2019
- **Strength:** Best price-to-capacity ratio, massive expansion options
- **Weakness:** Heavier units, app is basic
- **Battery tech:** LiFePO4 (all new models)
- **Warranty:** 2-4 years (model dependent)

Bluetti offers the most capacity per dollar and the most flexible expansion system. If you want to build a serious off-grid power system without breaking the bank, Bluetti is the way to go.

## Head-to-Head Comparison

### Charging Speed
1. **EcoFlow** — X-Stream charging is industry-leading. The DELTA 2 Max goes 0-80% in 43 minutes.
2. **Jackery** — Fast, but not EcoFlow-fast. Explorer 2000 Plus: 0-80% in ~2 hours.
3. **Bluetti** — Slowest of the three. AC200MAX: 0-80% in ~2.5 hours.

**Winner: EcoFlow** (by a mile)

### Battery Longevity
1. **Jackery** — 4,000 cycles on LiFePO4 models (best in class)
2. **Bluetti** — 3,500 cycles
3. **EcoFlow** — 3,000 cycles

**Winner: Jackery** (but all three are excellent)

### App & Smart Features
1. **EcoFlow** — Best app: real-time monitoring, remote control, scheduling, firmware updates
2. **Jackery** — Good app: basic monitoring and control
3. **Bluetti** — Basic app: monitoring only, occasional connectivity issues

**Winner: EcoFlow**

### Expandability
1. **Bluetti** — Up to 8,192Wh with expansion batteries on the AC200MAX
2. **EcoFlow** — Up to 6,144Wh with DELTA 2 Max + extra batteries
3. **Jackery** — Up to 12,000Wh with Explorer 2000 Plus ecosystem

**Winner: Jackery** (but only with their newest ecosystem)

### Price per Wh
1. **Bluetti** — ~$0.45/Wh (best value)
2. **EcoFlow** — ~$0.55/Wh
3. **Jackery** — ~$0.60/Wh

**Winner: Bluetti**

### Solar Panel Quality
1. **Jackery** — SolarSaga panels are the best in the industry
2. **EcoFlow** — Excellent bifacial panel technology
3. **Bluetti** — Good panels, but not class-leading

**Winner: Jackery**

## Which Brand Should You Choose?

**Choose Jackery if:** You value reliability above all else and don't mind paying a premium. Jackery products just work. Period. Best for people who need their generator to perform perfectly every single time — emergency preparedness, professional use, and frequent travelers.

**Choose EcoFlow if:** You want the latest technology and the fastest charging. EcoFlow is the Tesla of solar generators — always pushing boundaries. Best for tech enthusiasts, off-grid workers who need fast turnaround, and anyone who lives on their phone app.

**Choose Bluetti if:** You want the most capacity for your dollar and plan to expand your system over time. Bluetti's modular approach means you can start small and scale up. Best for budget-conscious buyers, off-grid homesteaders, and solar enthusiasts building a serious power system.
