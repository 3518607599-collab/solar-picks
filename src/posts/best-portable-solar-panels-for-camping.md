## What Makes a Good Camping Solar Panel?

- **Wattage** — 100W is the sweet spot for camping; enough to charge a power station in a reasonable time
- **Folded size** — Must fit in or on your backpack or car trunk
- **Weight** — Under 15 lbs for backpacking, under 25 lbs for car camping
- **Durability** — Waterproof rating (IP65+), rugged corners, quality connectors
- **Actual output** — Most panels deliver 60-80% of rated wattage in real conditions

## Pro Tips for Camping Solar

1. **Angle matters** — Point your panel directly at the sun. Even 15 degrees off reduces output by 20%.
2. **Keep it cool** — Hot panels lose 10-15% efficiency. Prop them up for airflow underneath.
3. **Morning and evening gold hours** — You get 60% of your daily solar production in the 4 hours around noon.
4. **Clean panels** — Dust and dirt reduce output by 5-10%. Wipe them down daily.
5. **Use the right cable** — Long cables cause voltage drop. Keep cable runs under 10 feet if possible.
