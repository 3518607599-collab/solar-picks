How Much Power Does a Home Need?

During an outage, you probably don't need to power everything. Focus on essentials:

Home Backup Setup Guide

The Transfer Switch Method (Recommended)

  1. Buy a transfer switch ($200-500 + installation)
  2. Connect your solar generator to the transfer switch
  3. Select which circuits to power during an outage
  4. When power goes out, flip the switch — your essentials are powered

This is the safest and most convenient method. An electrician can install a transfer switch in 2-3 hours.

The Extension Cord Method (Budget)

  1. Run extension cords from the generator to your essential devices
  2. Works fine for temporary outages
  3. Not practical for extended use
